**An open-source AI platform for trusted financial intelligence.**  

The Open Account is an open-source, AI-assisted accounting and financial insight platform designed to transform raw business transactions into **trusted financial truth, living budgets, and actionable management insights**. The platform focuses on the essence of accounting and financial management: **accuracy, explainability, and decision-useful information**â€”not black-box automation.

## ðŸ›  Proof-of-Concept Scope

The PoC demonstrates value with a **minimal but complete financial loop**:

### 1. AI-Assisted Bookkeeping
- Ingests bank transactions  
- Suggests account classifications with **confidence scores**  
- Maintains a **full audit trail**  
- Supports **human review and overrides**  

### 2. Financial Reporting & Analysis
- Generates **P&L and basic financial statements**  
- Performs **period-over-period comparisons**  
- Produces **plain-English explanations** tied to source data  

### 3. MIS Reporting
- Delivers **KPI dashboards**  
- Highlights **exceptions and anomalies**  
- Provides **role-based views** for business owners and accountants  

### 4. Budgeting & Forecasting
- Creates **baseline budgets** from historical data  
- Tracks **budget vs actual performance**  
- Produces **rolling short-term forecasts**  
- Supports **simple scenario analysis**
